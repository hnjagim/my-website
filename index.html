About Me

I specialize in Data Engineering, Automation in Business Analytics, and the design and deployment of systems for Spatial Analysis. My work emphasizes real-time analytics, predictive modeling, and monitoring and evaluation (M&E) to enhance strategic decision-making.

I help organizations harness the power of data by implementing geospatial systems, optimizing data pipelines, and extracting insights that support growth, innovation, and operational excellence.
