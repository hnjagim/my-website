About Me

I'm an experienced Data Scientist who has proved the ability to leverage information to produce commercial and organizational impact. I assist businesses and organizations with the implementation of geospatial systems, data collection, and the extraction of insights that identify growth opportunities, intervention areas, and ways to improve efficiency and strategy.

I specialize in Data Engineering, Automation in Business Analytics, and designing and implementing systems for Spatial Analysis. My work involves performing real-time analysis and generating predictive insights to support decision-making and Monitoring and Evaluation (M&E).
